At CodeCluster, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our platform.

Your information is used for the following purposes:

3. How We Protect Your Information

We take security seriously and implement various measures to protect your personal information, including encryption, secure payment gateways, and regular security audits. However, no method of data transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

However, we may share information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our platform or providing services. These third parties are obligated to keep your information confidential and secure.

5. Cookies

CodeCluster uses cookies to enhance your user experience. Cookies are small data files stored on your device that allow us to track usage and provide personalized content. You can choose to disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may limit some features of the platform.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time. If you wish to do so, please contact us at the details below. We will respond to your request as promptly as possible, in compliance with applicable laws.
